Insights from Usability Testing

  • 3/5 users reported that it takes them several seconds to figure out they need to press the buttons.
  • 5/5 suggested that more instructions are needed in order for them to immediately know what to do.
  • 1/5 had a problem because they had Caps Lock on, and the website doesn't accept uppercase letters.

Takeaways

By doing this project, I practiced my programming skills, especially when creating ripple effects using JavaScript. This is important because the same logic can be applied to many webpages, interactive screens, and even hardware, such as gaming keyboards.

Additionally, by conducting usability testing, I learned to effectively apply skills such as empathetic design thinking and strategic decision-making.
